NHL Stats Visulization App

This web app pulls hockey statistics from hockey-reference.com and creates a visualization of that data. Select what data you would like to visualize and what sort of visualization you would like to use. Currently, the options are limited to choosing a team and visualizing a statistic for that team over the course of 10 seasons from 2008-2018. More options will be added soon.

Note that a good Corsi For % and Fenwick For % is one that is above 50% (and in general higher is better). Similarly, a good PDO (which is the sum of the save % and shooting %) is one that is above 100% (and in general higher is better).
